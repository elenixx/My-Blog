Boston

I like Boston... I live a bit outside of Boston, in Wellesley (MA) so I go to Boston on the weekends when I have time with my friends. But I had been in Boston even before moving, which is where my love for the city comes from. My mother studied in Boston and we went back offten when we visited the US. She showed me and my sister around the city and told us about her experience there and her stories about the city. Now, though it is years later and I am no longer going into Boston with y mother, but with my friends, the city always reminds me of the summers I spent there with my mom and my sister and the memories we made together.
